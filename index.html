Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about TaxSaver AI

How does the AI find deductions I've missed?

Our AI system is trained on thousands of tax scenarios and constantly updated with the latest tax codes. It cross-references your financial data with known deductions and credits to identify savings opportunities that are often overlooked.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, we use bank-level 256-bit encryption to protect your data. We never sell your information to third parties, and all data is stored securely in compliance with financial industry regulations.

Can I use TaxSaver AI with my accountant?

Absolutely! Many of our users share their detailed tax reports with their accountants. Our professional PDF exports make it easy to collaborate with tax professionals.

What if I have a complex tax situation?

Our AI is designed to handle complex tax situations including investments, rental properties, and business ownership. For especially complex scenarios, our Business plan includes access to tax professionals for guidance.

How often is the tax information updated?

Our tax database is updated in real-time as new tax laws and regulations are passed. We have a team of tax experts who continuously monitor for changes that could affect our users.

We offer email support for all plans with a 24-hour response time. Professional and Business plans include live chat support during business hours. Business plan customers also get dedicated phone support.
